Who is Birdiedaboy?

Birdie is a Digital Artist based on Monterrey MX that works in diferents fields of Front End Development such as User Experience, User Interface, Accesability, Search Engine Optimization and Web Performance. He likes to solve mobility issues by creating digital tools to help citys become more friendly and accesible to all people by gathering data and using it to analyze problems and propose solutions. He has worked with private companys as well as goverment agencys helping them solve issues and optimizing their process.

He also does Interactive Art Installations, Video Mapping and Photography as tools for self expression.
